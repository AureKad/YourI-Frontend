Collective action adds up

When you donate to YI™, you invest in a future where culture is preserved and opportunities open up for people of all ethnicities and origins. Your donation helps us make this dream a reality.

Every Euro donated goes directly into the realisation of this project.

Why Should You Donate?

For every Euro donated to our organization, the impact within the network is equivalent to €3!

This means that your donation has a significant multiplier effect. For example, if you donate just $1 or €1 to a cause, the overall positive change or benefit that your donation generates within the network is valued at $3 or €3. This concept highlights how small contributions can lead to substantial outcomes when combined and leveraged effectively within a network.
